Intakes to Study in Bulgaria

Bulgarian universities typically offer two main intakes:

  • October: The primary intake for most undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including medicine and engineering.
  • February/March: A secondary intake is available at select universities for limited programs, mostly in business and technical fields.

Bulgaria Study Visa Requirements

Bulgaria has a clear and structured student visa process. To successfully obtain a student visa, applicants must fulfill the following key requirements:

  • Proof of admission to a recognized Bulgarian university
  • Valid passport and passport-sized photos
  • Proof of sufficient financial means to cover tuition and living costs
  • Health insurance valid in Bulgaria and the EU
  • Proof of accommodation in Bulgaria
  • Police clearance certificate from the home country

Note: Begin the visa application process several months before your intended intake to ensure timely approval.

Post-Study Work Permit (PSW) in Bulgaria

After completing their studies, international students in Bulgaria can apply for an extension of stay to look for work or continue further education. While Bulgaria does not have a formal post-study work visa like some other countries, graduates can apply for a long-term residence permit if they find employment. This can eventually lead to permanent residency and even EU-wide opportunities for mobility and career growth.

Tip: Start networking and job hunting 3-6 months before graduation for smoother transition.
